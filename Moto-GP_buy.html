MOTOGP TICKETS & HOSPITALITY

The MotoGP Championship is the premier motorcycle road racing series in the world. Sanctioned by the FIM and consisting of 27 drivers and 16 teams, the calendar takes in 19 different locations across the globe between March and November. Each race weekend take place over a 3 days, Friday is all about practice, Saturday is final practice & qualifying and Sunday is race day. In amongst these sessions, Moto2 and Moto3 races take place, plus for the second half of the European season; the MotoE class, providing a variety of live on-track action throughout the weekend.
