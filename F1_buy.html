FORMULA 1 TICKETS AND HOSPITALITY

The Formula 1 World Championship is widely regarded as the pinnacle of single-seater motorsport. Sanctioned by the FIA and consisting of 20 drivers and 10 teams, the calendar takes in 21 different locations across the globe between March and December, at a variety of purpose built and street circuit tracks.

Each race weekend takes place over a 3-days, Friday is all about practice, Saturday is final practice & qualifying and Sunday is race day. In amongst these sessions are a variety of races from other race series including F2, F3, Porsche SuperCup, and other local racing series’ providing a variety of live on-track action all day throughout the weekend.
